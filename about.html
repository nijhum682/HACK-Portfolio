About HACK

Accelerating Ideas into Hardware Reality

The Hardware Acceleration Club of KUET (HACK) is a student-led technical organization at Khulna University of Engineering and Technology that focuses on developing hands-on skills in hardware, embedded systems, robotics, and IoT. It provides a collaborative platform where students engage in practical learning through workshops, seminars, and project-based activities. The club encourages innovation and teamwork, helping members turn theoretical knowledge into real-world engineering solutions. HACK also organizes competitions, tech events, and project showcases to foster creativity and technical excellence. Overall, it plays a vital role in building a strong hardware-focused engineering community within KUET.

How can you join with HACK?

Joining HACK is a simple and engaging process open to all enthusiastic KUET students. Interested candidates typically need to keep an eye on official club announcements regarding recruitment drives and registration periods. During this time, applicants fill out a registration form and may go through a basic selection process such as an interview or task-based evaluation. The club looks for students who are passionate about hardware, innovation, and teamwork rather than just prior experience. Once selected, new members are welcomed into the community and can start participating in workshops, projects, and events.
