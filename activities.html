Our Activities

  • 🔵Hackathon 👉 24-hour coding challenge with mentors and prizes.
  • 🔵Cybersecurity Workshop 👉 hands-on sessions on ethical hacking.
  • 🔵Hardware Workshop 👉 hands-on session to design, build and test physical electronic systems and devices.
  • 🔵Graduate's Farewell 👉 held to honor graduating students and celebrate their contributions in HACK.
  • 🔵Fresher's Orientation 👉 introductory program for freshers to understand club's environment, resources and expectations.
  • 🔵Intra-University Hardware Competition 👉 arranged to design and build hardware solutions showcasing technical skills of University students.
